Aadhaar Card Status

To check the Aadhaar Card Status, you can use the following methods:

Online Via My Aadhaar Portal

  • Visit the UIDAI myAadhaar Portal ‚Äì https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/.

Check Aadhaar Status via SMS

To check your Aadhaar enrollment or update status via SMS:

  • Use the EID (Enrolment ID) from your acknowledgment slip or URN (Update Request Number).

Check Aadhar Card Status Without Enrollment Number

If you forget your enrollment number, you can retrieve it using the following steps:

  • Visit the myAadhaar Portal ‚Äì https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/.
  • Click on ‚ÄúRetrieve EID / Aadhaar number.‚Äù
  • Choose whether to retrieve the Aadhaar number or the Enrollment ID (EID).
  • Enter your name, mobile number, and email ID, and captcha.
  • Enter the OTP received on your registered number for verification.
  • After verification, your Enrollment Number or Aadhaar Number will be sent to your registered mobile number.
