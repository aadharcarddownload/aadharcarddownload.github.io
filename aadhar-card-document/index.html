AADHAR Card Documents Required

Documents Required for Aadhaar Card. Find out what documents are needed for Aadhaar Card. Useful information and list of documents for those who are going to enroll for Aadhar Card.

आधार डाउनलोड आधार अपडेट आधार स्टेटस चेक MyAadhaar Portal

The UIDAI has provided a 12-digit unique Aadhaar number to all citizens of India. The number is linked to the citizen’s basic demographic and personal information, photograph, 10 fingerprints, and iris scans, which are stored in a centralised database.

Aadhar card is important for every individual who is a citizen of India. As per the latest information, 84 crore have been enrolled in the project and 40 crore are yet to be enrolled. Enrolling for AADHAAR is not compulsory. However, agencies and service providers may choose to use AADHAAR in their system, in which case you may be required to get the AADHAAR to use their services.

AADHAAR Card Documents

Every applicant is required to provide three supporting documents related to their AADHAAR Card: one for Proof of Identity, one for Proof of Address, and one for Proof of Date of Birth.

If you don’t have these documents, don’t worry! You can still enroll as long as your name is listed in the family entitlement document. The Head of the Family needs to enroll first with valid documents and can then introduce the other family members. If no one in your family has valid documents, an Introducer at the enrollment center can assist you.

You need to fill out the Aadhar enrollment form and attach a photocopy of your Proof of Address and Proof of Identity.

Proof of Identity Documents

  • Passport
  • PAN Card
  • Ration / PDS Photo Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving License
  • Government Photo ID Cards / PSU ID
  • Photo ID from Recognized Educational Institution
  • Arms License
  • Photo Bank ATM/Credit Card
  • Pensioner Photo Card
  • Freedom Fighter Photo Card
  • Kissan Photo Passbook
  • Address Card with Photo from Dept. of Posts
  • Certificate with Photo from Gazetted Officer/Tehsildar
  • Disability ID/Handicapped Certificate
  • Marriage Certificate
  • Proof of Marriage issued by Registrar
  • Gazette Notification
  • Legal Name Change Certificate

Proof of Address Documents

  • Ration Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving License
  • Passport
  • Bank/PO Statement or Passbook
  • Electricity, Water, or Landline Bill (last 3 months)
  • Credit Card Statement (last 3 months)
  • Insurance Policy
  • Signed Letter with Photo from Bank/Company/Educational Institution
  • NREGS Job Card
  • Arms License
  • Pensioner Card
  • Freedom Fighter Card
  • Kissan Passbook
  • Certificate of Address from MP/MLA/Officer
  • Village Panchayat Certificate (rural areas)
  • Income Tax Assessment Order
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate
  • Sale/Lease/Rent Agreement
  • Address Card from Dept. of Posts
  • Caste/Domicile Certificate with Photo
  • Disability ID/Govt. Handicapped Certificate
  • Gas Connection Bill (last 3 months)

Proof of Relationship Documents

  • PDS Card
  • MNREGA Job Card
  • CGHS / ECHS / ESIC Medical Card
  • Pension Card
  • Army Canteen Card
  • Passport
  • Birth Certificate from Govt. Body
  • Other Government Issued Family Entitlement Document

Proof of Date of Birth Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • SSLC Book / Certificate
  • Passport
  • Date of Birth Certificate by Group A Gazetted Officer
