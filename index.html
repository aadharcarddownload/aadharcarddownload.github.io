Aadhar Card Download - Access your Aadhaar (Unique Identification Authority of India)

The Unique Identification Authority of India UIDAI issues Aadhaar cards as an essential identification document to residents under Government of India mandate. The 12-digit identification number on Aadhaar originates from biometric checkpoints combined with resident demographics. Indian residents can use their Aadhaar card as identification proof of both identity and residential address nationwide for accessing government subsidies and financial transactions and mobile services and online web services. Digital governance receives its improvement through the card which connects basic services and maintains transparency and guards against identity theft.

Note: As per the Aadhaar Act, e-Aadhaar is as valid as the physical Aadhaar card for all purposes.   • If you have Aadhaar number then enter it, else use EID or VID.
During application EID (Enrolment ID) provides a 28-digit tracking code but UID (Unique Identification Number) stands as a 12-digit proof of identity which receives completion approval.

  • Now Click on the Verify Button then Click on the Download button.
  • Your Aadhaar card will be downloaded to your device.

    What is Aadhaar Card?

    The UIDAI issues Aadhaar cards as mandatory identification documents which contain specific 12-digit numbers to verify that the holder is a resident of India. The UIDAI began operations in January 2009 to distribute more than 1.98 billion Aadhaar numbers as of December 2025.

    By associating with a 12-digit Aadhaar number every individual acquires a digital identity that enables either identification through biometric data (fingerprint and iris scans) or by receiving a one-time password (OTP) to their registered mobile number.

    What is e-Aadhaar?

    E-Aadhaar represents as electronic version of your Aadhaar card holding a digital signature from Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI). UIDAI digital signatures authenticate your entire Aadhaar card contents starting from the 12-digit identifier and personal information and photograph and biometric records.

    E-Aadhaar holds equivalent validity status to physical Aadhaar and users can access it through the UIDAI website or mAadhaar application.

    Key Features of e-Aadhaar:

    • Digital format: Invalidity and accessibility through digital methods are possible with your Aadhaar information.
    • Password protected: The PDF file contains a password protection system to maintain privacy.
    • Legally valid: The digital form of Aadhaar maintains complete legal validity which equals the physical paper form of the card.
    • Masked Aadhaar option: The Masked Aadhaar feature enables you to obtain an Aadhaar document displayed with the initial 8 digits of your Aadhaar number obscured.

    You can perform identity verification as well as access government services and official procedures by using E-Aadhaar in the same manner as the physical card.
